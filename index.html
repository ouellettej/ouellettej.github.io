HACTC Curriclulm Design

I joined the HACTC as an instructor in February of 2022 and was tasked with restarting their Information Technology program, which had been dormant for several years. I undertook a complete redesign of the curriculum informed by meetings with local employers and postsecondary education providers. The resulting curriculum thoroughly prepares students for immediate career success or further education and training in the IT industry.

For more details see the program pages for my Information Technology and Software Engineering classes on the HACTC website.
